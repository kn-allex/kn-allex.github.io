Mojito

John Doe 1 serving PT5M PT10M
150 calories
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 6 leaves fresh mint
  • Soda water
  • 1 lime, halved
  • 2 ounces white rum
  • Ice cubes

Muddle the sugar, mint leaves, and a little club soda in a glass.

Squeeze the juice from both halves of the lime into the glass, dropping one half into the glass.

Add the rum, stir, and fill the glass with ice cubes.

Top off with soda water, garnish with the remaining lime half, and enjoy!
